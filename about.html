No 2 Ethics

I believe that the future of successful innovation lies in being expectedly unexpected.

Recently I've been learning about human centered design-- specifically how everyone is the protagonist of their own story and how products are simply tools along that journey. When I design, I strive to create something that solves an unrealized problem in an intuitive and adoptable way. I want my work to be recognizable for its seamless efficency between form and function, as well as for offering a new solution to a legitimate need.

No 3 Coursework

The Iovine and Young Academy of Arts, Technology, and the Business of Innovation.

The Iovine and Young Academy is a recently creaed program at USC that integrates the areas of design, entrepreneurship, and computer science. While we delve deeper into all the fields individually, we also prioritize communication at the intersection.

Notable Classes

  • Innovator's Forum: Weekly concept pitches to leaders in diverse disciplines and industries.
  • Design Consulting: Diagnosing problems and applying design-based solutions on behalf of organizations.Personal focus on human centered design and user research.
  • Dirsuptive Innovation: Analyis of critical approaches to social and cultural changes in science, design, and business.
  • Designing for Experiences: Data visualization through various mediums including web apps, arduino, maps, and textiles.
